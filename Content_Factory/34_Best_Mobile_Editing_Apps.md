# Best Free Video Editing Apps for iPhone and Android

In 2026, you don't need a computer to be a high-end content creator. The latest smartphones are more powerful than the laptops of five years ago, and mobile editing apps have evolved from simple "trim and crop" tools into full-featured professional suites. Whether you are shooting a vlog on the go or editing a quick TikTok at a cafe, the right mobile app is essential.

Here are the best free video editing apps for iPhone and Android in 2026.

## 1. CapCut (The Viral King)
CapCut is the most popular mobile editor in the world for a reason. Owned by ByteDance (the creators of TikTok), it is designed specifically for social media creators.
*   **Best Features:** AI-generated captions, trending transition templates, and "Body Effects" that track your movement. It also has an incredible background removal tool that works without a green screen.
*   **Pros:** Extremely easy to use; built-in music library.
*   **Cons:** Some of the best features are now locked behind a "Pro" subscription.

## 2. DaVinci Resolve for iPad (The Pro Choice)
If you have an iPad (specifically an M-series iPad), you can now run the *actual* DaVinci Resolve software on your tablet.
*   **Best Features:** Professional color grading wheels, the "Cut" page for fast editing, and high-end AI tools like "Magic Mask."
*   **Pros:** It’s a professional-grade tool for free; project files can be shared with the desktop version.
*   **Cons:** Not available for Android; requires a very powerful iPad to run smoothly.

## 3. VN Video Editor (The Clean Alternative)
VN is the favorite of many professional vlogger-style creators because it doesn't have watermarks and offers a "desktop-like" timeline on a phone.
*   **Best Features:** "Curve" speed ramping (for cinematic slow motion), multi-track editing, and the ability to import your own fonts and music.
*   **Pros:** No watermarks; very clean and professional UI.
*   **Cons:** Fewer "AI" bells and whistles than CapCut.

## 4. LumaFusion (The Powerhouse)
While LumaFusion is a paid app, it is often on sale or available in a lite version. It is widely considered the best multi-track editor for mobile.
*   **Best Features:** Up to 6 video/audio tracks, professional keyframing, and advanced color correction.
*   **Pros:** The closest thing to Premiere Pro on a phone.
*   **Cons:** It has a steeper learning curve than CapCut or VN.

## 6. Tips for Mobile Editing Success
*   **Use a Stylus:** If you are editing on a phone, a cheap stylus can help you make more precise cuts and keyframe adjustments.
*   **Proxy Editing:** Many mobile apps allow you to edit with low-res versions of your 4K phone footage to save battery and reduce lag.
*   **Clear Cache:** Mobile editors eat up storage fast. Regularly go into the app settings and "Clear Cache" to reclaim space on your phone.
