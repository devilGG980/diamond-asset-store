# Best Teleprompter Apps for Creators

## Introduction
As we’ve discussed, a teleprompter is one of the most powerful tools for increasing your "Professionalism" and "Efficiency" on camera. However, a physical teleprompter rig is only half the battle. You need high-quality "Teleprompter Software" to control the speed, the font, and the "Smart Features" that make reading look natural. In 2026, teleprompter apps have evolved to include "Voice-Tracking" (where the text follows your speech) and "AI-Refining" (where the app simplifies your script on the fly). Whether you’re using an iPhone, an Android, or a laptop, there is an app designed to make you look like a seasoned broadcaster. This guide reviews the best teleprompter apps for creators in 2026.

## 1. The "Smart" Choice: PromptSmart+
PromptSmart is the pioneer of "VoiceTrack" technology, and it remains the best app for creators who want a "Natural" flow.
### Why it's Great
It uses speech recognition to "Listen" to you while you speak. The text only scrolls when you say the words. If you go off-script to share an anecdote, the prompter "Waits" for you. If you speed up, it speeds up. It removes the "Stress" of trying to "Chase" the text.
### Platforms
Available on iOS, Android, and Web.

## 2. The "Mobile" Specialist: Teleprompter for Video
For many creators, the smartphone *is* the camera and the prompter at the same time.
### Why it's Great
This app is designed specifically for "Selfie-style" recording. It places the script directly next to the camera lens on your phone screen, ensuring your "Eye-line" is perfect. It also features "Automatic Captions"—it uses your script to generate captions for the video as you record it, saving you hours of editing for TikTok and Reels.
### Platforms
iOS and Android.

## 3. The "Free" Hero: Nano Teleprompter
If you’re on a tight budget and need a simple, reliable tool, Nano is the best option.
### Why it's Great
It features a "Floating Widget" mode. You can open your camera app or a Zoom call, and the teleprompter script "Floats" over the top. It’s minimalist, fast, and doesn't require a subscription for the basic features.
### Platforms
Android.

## 4. The "Webcam" Companion: Elgato Camera Hub
If you use an Elgato webcam or the Elgato Prompter rig, their "Camera Hub" software is the most integrated experience.
### Why it's Great
It allows you to turn any window on your computer (a script, a browser, or even a Discord chat) into a "Prompter Feed." It is perfectly synced with Elgato’s hardware, allowing you to change the text speed using a "Stream Deck" or a mouse wheel.
### Platforms
Windows and Mac.

## 5. The "Professional" Standard: Teleprompter Premium
If you use a physical "Glass-and-Hood" rig with an iPad, this is the app the pros use.
### Why it's Great
It features "Mirror Mode" (which flips the text so it looks correct on the glass) and "Remote Control" (allowing you to use your phone or a Bluetooth keyboard to control the speed from across the room). It also supports importing scripts directly from Google Drive, Dropbox, and Word.
### Platforms
iOS and Mac.

## 7. How to Choose: The "Rig" Filter
### If you are shooting "Selfie-style" on your phone:
Go with **Teleprompter for Video**. The eye-line optimization is a lifesaver.
### If you are using a "Real" camera with a glass rig:
Go with **Teleprompter Premium** or **PromptSmart+**.
### If you are doing "Live Streams" or "Zoom Presentations":
Go with **Elgato Camera Hub** or **Nano Teleprompter**.
