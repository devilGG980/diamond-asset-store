# How to Unblock Someone on Facebook: Quick and Easy Guide

Made amends with someone you previously blocked? Learning **how to unblock someone on Facebook** restores your connection and allows you to interact again. This comprehensive guide provides step-by-step instructions for unblocking people on Facebook across all devices.

Whether you blocked someone during a disagreement or for privacy reasons, Facebook makes it easy to reverse that decision. We'll show you exactly **how to unblock someone on Facebook** on mobile and desktop, explain what happens when you unblock, and answer common questions about the unblocking process.

## Understanding Facebook's Blocking Feature

Before learning **how to unblock someone on Facebook**, understand what blocking does:

### What Happens When You Block Someone

Blocking creates these restrictions:

- **No profile visibility**: You both disappear from each other's Facebook
- **Unfriending**: Facebook automatically unfriends blocked users
- **No messages**: You can't send messages to each other
- **No tagging**: Neither person can tag the other
- **Hidden posts**: You won't see each other's posts or comments
- **Search invisibility**: You can't find each other through Facebook search

### Why People Unblock Others

Common reasons for learning **how to unblock someone on Facebook**:

- Resolving conflicts and rebuilding relationships
- Unintentional blocking errors
- Changed circumstances or perspectives
- Professional networking needs
- Family reconciliation
- Mutual friends requesting reconnection

## How to Unblock Someone on Facebook on Desktop

Follow these detailed steps to **unblock someone on Facebook** using your computer:

### Step 1: Access Facebook Settings

1. Log into **Facebook** on your web browser
2. Click the **down arrow** icon in the top-right corner
3. Select **Settings & Privacy** from the dropdown menu
4. Click **Settings** to open your account settings

### Step 2: Navigate to Blocking Settings

1. In the left sidebar menu, find and click **Blocking**
2. Alternatively, search for "Blocking" in the settings search bar
3. You'll see your **Block users** section with a list of blocked people

### Step 3: Locate the Person

1. Scroll through your **Block users** list
2. Find the specific person you want to unblock
3. Each blocked user appears with their name and profile picture

### Step 4: Unblock the Person

1. Click the **Unblock** button next to their name
2. Facebook displays a confirmation dialog
3. Confirm your decision by clicking **Unblock** again
4. The person immediately removes from your blocked list

**Important**: After you **unblock someone on Facebook**, you must wait 48 hours before you can block them again.

## How to Unblock Someone on Facebook on Mobile

The mobile process for **how to unblock someone on Facebook** is equally straightforward:

### For iPhone (iOS):

#### Step 1: Open Facebook Settings

1. Launch the **Facebook app** on your iPhone
2. Tap the **menu icon** (three horizontal lines) in the bottom-right corner
3. Scroll down and tap **Settings & Privacy**
4. Tap **Settings**

#### Step 2: Access Blocking Settings

1. Scroll down to the **Audience and Visibility** section
2. Tap **Blocking** to view your blocked users list
3. You'll see everyone currently blocked

#### Step 3: Unblock the Person

1. Find the person you want to unblock in the list
2. Tap **Unblock** next to their name
3. Confirm by tapping **Unblock** again when prompted
4. They're immediately removed from your blocked list

### For Android:

The Android process mirrors the iPhone steps:

1. Open the **Facebook app**
2. Tap the **menu icon** (three horizontal lines) in the top-right
3. Tap **Settings & Privacy** → **Settings**
4. Navigate to **Blocking** under Audience and Visibility
5. Find the person in your blocked users list
6. Tap **Unblock** next to their name
7. Confirm the unblock action

## What Happens After You Unblock Someone?

Understanding the implications helps you decide whether to **unblock someone on Facebook**:

### Immediate Effects

**Profile visibility restored**: You can now visit each other's profiles based on privacy settings

**Search capability**: You can find each other through Facebook search again

**Messaging possible**: You can send messages again through Messenger

**Tagging allowed**: Both parties can tag each other in posts and photos

### What Doesn't Happen Automatically

**Not automatic friends**: Unblocking doesn't restore the friendship; you must send a new friend request

**Previous messages**: Old Messenger conversations may remain archived

**Past interactions**: Previous likes, comments, and tags don't restore

**Privacy settings**: Individual privacy settings still apply as configured

### The 48-Hour Rule

After you **unblock someone on Facebook**:

- You **cannot re-block** them for 48 hours
- This prevents abuse of the blocking feature
- Consider carefully before unblocking
- The restriction applies from the moment you unblock

## How to Add Someone Back After Unblocking

Unblocking doesn't restore friendships. Here's how to reconnect:

### Sending a Friend Request

1. Search for the person using Facebook search
2. Navigate to their profile
3. Click or tap **Add Friend**
4. Wait for them to accept your request
5. Once accepted, you're friends again

### Reconnection Considerations

Before sending a friend request after unblocking:

- **Consider the relationship**: Ensure both parties want reconnection
- **Send a message**: Explain why you're reaching out
- **Respect boundaries**: Accept if they don't accept your request
- **Adjust expectations**: The friendship may differ from before

## Unblocking vs. Unfriending vs. Restricting

Facebook offers several ways to manage connections:

### Blocking

**Most restrictive option**:
- Complete mutual invisibility
- No interaction possible
- Requires unblocking to restore any connection

### Unfriending

**Moderate option**:
- Removes friendship but maintains some visibility
- Can still see public posts
- Can send messages (unless restricted separately)
- Learn more in our guide on managing Facebook connections

### Restricting

**Subtle option**:
- Remain friends but limit what they see
- They see only public posts
- Useful alternative to blocking
- They won't know they're restricted

## How to Unblock Someone on Facebook Messenger

Messenger blocking is separate from Facebook blocking:

### Unblocking in Messenger

1. Open the **Messenger app**
2. Tap your **profile picture** in the top-left
3. Scroll to **Privacy & Safety**
4. Tap **Blocked accounts**
5. Find the person you want to unblock
6. Tap **Unblock on Messenger**
7. Confirm the action

**Note**: Someone blocked only on Messenger can still interact with you on Facebook. Someone blocked on Facebook is automatically blocked on Messenger.

## Troubleshooting Unblocking Issues

### Can't Find Someone to Unblock

**Problem**: The person isn't in your blocked list

**Solutions**:
- Check spelling carefully
- Search using different name variations
- They may have deactivated or deleted their account
- You might have restricted rather than blocked them

### Unblock Button Not Working

**Problem**: Clicking unblock doesn't work

**Solutions**:
- Refresh the page or restart the app
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser or device
- Update the Facebook app to the latest version
- Wait a few minutes and try again

### Can't Send Friend Request After Unblocking

**Problem**: Unable to send friend request immediately after unblocking

**Solutions**:
- Wait a few minutes for systems to update
- Refresh the page
- Check if they've blocked friend requests from non-friends
- Verify their account is active

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will they know I unblocked them?

No, Facebook doesn't send notifications when you **unblock someone on Facebook**. They'll only know if they try to view your profile and succeed.

### Can I unblock someone and immediately re-block them?

No, Facebook enforces a 48-hour waiting period before you can re-block someone after unblocking them.

### Does unblocking restore our friendship?

No, unblocking only removes the block. You must send a new friend request to become friends again.

### What about old messages?

Previous Messenger conversations may still exist in archived messages, but no new conversation starts automatically.

### Can I see what they posted while blocked?

After unblocking, you can see their current and future posts (based on privacy settings), but you might not see everything posted during the block period.

### How long does unblocking take?

Unblocking is immediate. However, it may take a few minutes for all Facebook systems to fully update.
