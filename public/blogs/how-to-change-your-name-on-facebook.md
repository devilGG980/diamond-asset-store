# How to Change Your Name on Facebook: Complete Step-by-Step Guide

Want to update your Facebook identity? Learning **how to change your name on Facebook** is essential whether you're updating after marriage, correcting a typo, or simply want to use your preferred name. This comprehensive guide walks you through every step of the process.

Facebook's name change policies have specific rules, but the process is straightforward when you know exactly what to do. We'll cover **how to change your name on Facebook** for both mobile and desktop, explain Facebook's name requirements, and answer common questions about name changes.

## Facebook Name Change Requirements

Before learning **how to change your name on Facebook**, understand Facebook's policies:

### Facebook's Name Rules

Facebook requires users to:

- Use the name they go by in everyday life
- Provide authentic information
- Avoid symbols, numbers, unusual capitalization, or punctuation
- Refrain from using offensive or suggestive words
- Not include professional titles (Dr., Rev., etc.)
- Use a single language for your name
- Avoid character substitutions or random characters

### Name Change Limitations

Facebook implements these restrictions:

- You can change your name on Facebook **once every 60 days**
- Repeated change requests may trigger verification requirements
- Names deemed inauthentic may require documentation
- Accounts violating policies face restricted name change abilities

## How to Change Your Name on Facebook on Desktop

Follow these detailed steps to **change your name on Facebook** using a computer:

### Step 1: Access Facebook Settings

1. Log into your Facebook account on a web browser
2. Click your **profile picture** in the top-right corner
3. Select **Settings & privacy** from the dropdown menu
4. Click **Settings** to open your account settings

### Step 2: Navigate to Name Settings

1. In the left sidebar, locate **Personal Information** or **General**
2. Find the **Name** section at the top of the settings
3. Click **Edit** next to your current name

### Step 3: Enter Your New Name

1. Enter your **First Name** in the appropriate field
2. Add your **Middle Name** (optional)
3. Enter your **Last Name** in the surname field
4. Review the preview showing how your name appears to others

### Step 4: Review and Save Changes

1. Check the preview carefully to ensure correct display
2. Enter your Facebook **password** when prompted
3. Click **Review Change** to proceed
4. Click **Save Changes** to finalize the update

Facebook reviews your name change and typically approves it within minutes, though some requests may take up to 24 hours.

## How to Change Your Name on Facebook on Mobile

The mobile process for **how to change your name on Facebook** varies slightly:

### For iPhone (iOS):

#### Step 1: Open Facebook Settings

1. Launch the **Facebook app** on your iPhone
2. Tap the **menu icon** (three horizontal lines) in the bottom-right corner
3. Scroll down and tap **Settings & Privacy**
4. Tap **Settings**

#### Step 2: Access Personal Information

1. Tap **Personal Information** in the settings menu
2. Tap **Name** to edit your name

#### Step 3: Update Your Name

1. Enter your new **First Name**
2. Add your **Middle Name** if desired
3. Enter your new **Last Name**
4. Review the preview

#### Step 4: Confirm Changes

1. Tap **Review Change**
2. Enter your **password**
3. Tap **Save Changes**

### For Android:

The process for Android mirrors the iPhone steps:

1. Open the **Facebook app**
2. Tap the **menu icon** (three horizontal lines)
3. Tap **Settings & Privacy** → **Settings**
4. Select **Personal Information**
5. Tap **Name**
6. Enter your new name details
7. Review and **Save Changes** after entering your password

## Adding Additional Name Options

Facebook allows customization beyond just **how to change your name on Facebook**:

### Alternate Name

Add nicknames, maiden names, or other names people know you by:

1. Go to your **Settings** → **Personal Information**
2. Click **Add other names** or **Alternate name**
3. Select the name type: Nickname, Maiden name, Alternate spelling, etc.
4. Enter the alternate name
5. Choose visibility options (show at top of profile, or just in About section)
6. **Save** your changes

### Name Pronunciation

Help people say your name correctly:

1. Navigate to your **Profile**
2. Click **About** → **Details About You**
3. Add **Name pronunciation** in audio or text format
4. **Save** the pronunciation guide

## Troubleshooting Name Change Issues

### "Your Request Couldn't Be Processed"

If you receive this error when trying to **change your name on Facebook**:

**Solution 1**: Wait 60 days if you recently changed your name

**Solution 2**: Ensure your name meets Facebook's requirements

**Solution 3**: Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again

**Solution 4**: Try a different browser or switch between mobile and desktop

### Name Change Request Under Review

Facebook flagged your request for manual review:

**What this means**: Your name requires verification before approval

**Timeline**: Reviews typically complete within 24-48 hours

**Action required**: Wait for Facebook's response; you may need to provide identification

### Name Change Rejected

If Facebook rejects your name change:

**Review the rejection reason**: Facebook typically explains why

**Check compliance**: Ensure your name follows Facebook's policies

**Provide documentation**: Submit identification if requested

**Appeal the decision**: Use Facebook's appeal process if you believe the rejection was incorrect

## Verification Requirements

Sometimes Facebook requires verification when you **change your name on Facebook**:

### When Verification Is Needed

Facebook accepts these forms of identification:

- Driver's license

- Birth certificate

1. Facebook will prompt you to verify if needed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does it take to change your name on Facebook?

Most name changes process immediately or within minutes. Flagged requests may take 24-48 hours for review.

### Can I change my name back?

Yes, but you must wait 60 days between name changes unless Facebook grants an exception.

### Will changing my name affect my account?

No, changing your name doesn't affect your friends, posts, photos, or any other account data. Everything updates with your new name.

### What if I made a typo?

If you notice an error immediately after changing your name, you'll need to wait 60 days or contact Facebook support for an exception.

### Can I use a single name on Facebook?

Facebook generally requires both first and last names. Single-name accounts need special verification and may not be approved.

### How do I change someone else's name?

You can't change another person's name, but you can report inappropriate names to Facebook.
