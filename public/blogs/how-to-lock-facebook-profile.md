## How to Lock Facebook Profile on Mobile

The mobile app provides the easiest method to **lock your Facebook profile**:

### For Android Users:

#### Step 1: Access Your Profile

1. Open the **Facebook app** on your Android device
2. Tap your **profile picture** in the top-left or your name to open your profile
3. Ensure you're viewing your own profile, not someone else's

#### Step 2: Open Profile Lock Settings

1. Look for the three-dot **menu button** or **lock icon** near your profile picture
2. Tap the menu to see available options
3. Select **Lock Profile** from the menu options

#### Step 3: Review Lock Features

Facebook displays what locking your profile does:

- Shows how your profile appears to non-friends
- Explains the privacy restrictions applied
- Confirms you can unlock anytime

#### Step 4: Confirm Profile Lock

1. Tap **Lock Your Profile** to confirm
2. Facebook processes your request immediately
3. Your profile is now locked to non-friends

### For iPhone (iOS) Users:

The iOS process mirrors Android:

1. Open the **Facebook app**
2. Tap your **profile picture** or navigate to your profile
3. Tap the **three-dot menu** or **lock icon**
4. Select **Lock Profile**
5. Review the information presented
6. Tap **Lock Your Profile** to confirm

## How to Lock Facebook Profile on Desktop

While the mobile app is recommended, you can achieve similar privacy on desktop:

### Adjust Privacy Settings on Computer

Facebook desktop doesn't have a one-tap "Lock Profile" button, but you can manually configure equivalent privacy:

#### Step 1: Access Privacy Settings

1. Log into **Facebook** on your web browser
2. Click the **down arrow** in the top-right corner
3. Select **Settings & Privacy**
4. Click **Privacy Shortcuts**

#### Step 2: Configure Post Privacy

1. Click **Privacy Checkup** or **Privacy Settings**
2. Find **Who can see your future posts**
3. Select **Friends** instead of Public
4. This restricts all future posts to friends only

#### Step 3: Limit Past Posts

1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy**
2. Find **Limit Past Posts**
3. Click **Limit Old Posts** to change all public posts to friends-only
4. Confirm the action

#### Step 4: Control Profile Visibility

1. Navigate to **Privacy Settings**
2. Adjust **Who can see your friends list** to Friends or Only Me
3. Set **Who can look you up using email/phone** to Friends
4. Configure **Who can send friend requests** as needed

#### Step 5: Manage Tagged Post Settings

1. Go to **Timeline and Tagging** settings
2. Enable **Review posts you're tagged in before they appear**
3. Adjust who can see tagged posts appropriately

While these steps don't replicate the exact mobile "Lock Profile" feature, they achieve similar privacy protection.

Yes, people can still find you in search, but they'll see limited information when they visit your profile.

No, when you **lock your Facebook profile**, tagged photos remain hidden from non-friends.

### Can I still tag others when locked?

Yes, locking your profile doesn't affect your ability to tag friends or interact normally.

### Will friends know I locked my profile?

No, Facebook doesn't notify friends when you **lock your Facebook profile**. They won't notice any changes.

### Does locking affect Messenger?
